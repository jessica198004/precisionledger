About Precision Ledger

At Precision Ledger Company LLC, we provide reliable bookkeeping and administrative support services designed specifically for small businesses. Founded by Jessica Callahan, a financial operations professional with over 15 years of experience in banking, compliance, and small business bookkeeping, Precision Ledger was created to bring clarity and structure to growing organizations.

Our approach is simple

Accurate records. Organized systems. Clear communication.

We understand that many business owners don’t need a full accounting department — they need dependable support that keeps their books clean and their operations running smoothly. That’s where we come in.

How it works
  1. Quick consult: we learn your business and your current system.
  2. Onboarding: connect accounts, confirm categories, set reporting.
  3. Monthly close: reconcile, review, and deliver reports on schedule.
Typical turnaround

Monthly close within 5–10 business days after month-end (depending on account volume and client responsiveness).
